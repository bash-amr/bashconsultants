Get numbers you can act on. We pull data out of your accounting system, your enterprise resource planning (ERP) software, and the spreadsheets in between, then build reports and dashboards your team can run without waiting on anyone.

## The problem we solve

Most growing businesses keep their real numbers in spreadsheets that don't reconcile, copied by hand from one system to another. Reports take days, two people get different totals from the same question, and nobody fully trusts the answer. The fix isn't a bigger spreadsheet — it's one place where the data is clean, current, and consistent.

## What we do

- **Data architecture and modeling** — a clear design for where your data lives and how it fits together, mapped to the questions your business actually asks.
- **Data warehousing** — a single, organized store that pulls from your ERP, accounting, point-of-sale (POS), and eCommerce systems so every report draws from the same source.
- **Integration and ETL** — extract, transform, and load (ETL) pipelines that move data on a schedule, clean it, and check it, so you stop copying numbers by hand.
- **Business intelligence (BI) dashboards** — interactive reports in Microsoft Power BI (or Tableau) that update on their own and let people drill into the detail behind a number.
- **Financial and manufacturing analytics** — general-ledger reporting, cost-accounting variance, accounts-receivable (AR) aging, inventory analytics, multi-entity consolidation, and key-performance-indicator (KPI) dashboards built from real finance and operations experience.

## Tools and platforms

- **BI and reporting** — Microsoft Power BI (our primary tool), Tableau.
- **Databases** — Microsoft SQL Server for modeling, reporting, and stored logic.
- **Cloud data warehouses** — Snowflake, Amazon Redshift, Google BigQuery, and Azure Synapse.
- **ETL and integration** — SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS) and Azure Data Factory.
- **Data work** — Python and SQL for transformation, validation, and one-off analysis.

We pick tools to fit your budget and what you already run, not the other way around.

## Who it's for

- A **manufacturer** still building production and cost reports by hand each month, who wants dashboards for output, variance, and inventory instead. Our background is in manufacturing analytics and financial reporting, so this is familiar ground.
- A **multi-location retailer** reconciling POS, eCommerce, and accounting data across stores, who needs one trustworthy view of sales and margin.

If you recognize your business here — "the spreadsheets don't tie out" or "month-end close takes days when it should take hours" — that's the moment to call.

## How we work

1. **Discovery.** A free conversation about your systems, your reports today, and the questions you wish you could answer.
2. **Assess.** We map where your data lives, how clean it is, and where the manual steps and mismatches happen.
3. **Design.** We model a warehouse and reporting structure that fits your data and your team.
4. **Build.** We set up the ETL pipelines, load the warehouse, and build the dashboards and reports.
5. **Train.** We hand it over with training so your team can run, read, and extend the reports on their own.

## What to expect

We work in honest ranges, not guarantees. A focused first dashboard — one subject area, connected to live data — typically runs **2–5 weeks**. A full warehouse with several integrated sources and a set of dashboards usually runs **6–14 weeks** depending on how many systems are involved and how clean the data is. We scope a phase, deliver something useful, then decide the next step together.

The founder brings more than a decade of data and financial-systems work in manufacturing, so the reporting and accounting detail gets handled correctly the first time.
